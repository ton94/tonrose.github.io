Company Background

An Advetising company inside Alabang Hills, Muntinlupa. This is my first job, before ZPMI (Zion's Praise Music Inc.). Here I work as a full-time Graphic Artist/Designer. They have big clients like HGS (an Indian BPO company), Mondelez Philippines, Minute Burger, Virlanie etc. Featured in this section are my works and initial concept design mostly for selected Mondelez brands and other clients of EGG Design.

Cheeseanything.com (Eden) | UI icons & Badges

Cheeseanything.com is a social site from Eden Cheese wherein people can share and upload different recipes especially with cheese. This icons are used for accessibility of the site while badges serves as something that can encourage users to collect more of it in order to gain prizes (e.g. a prize awaits for those people with many followers or many likes).
