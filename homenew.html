Kaibigan (Recital Theme 2017) | Zion's Praise Music Inc.

'Kaibigan' in Filipino means friendship and they made it as a theme this year because Music bound friendships together and make it last. The use of old/pre-hispanic Philippine letters called "Baybayin" is incorporated in the theme to promote nationalism in music. The theme is used in banners, posters, programmes, website and other promotional materials.
